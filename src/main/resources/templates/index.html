Do You Know Endodontics?

Going to the dentist should be a part of everyone's routine. It is recommended that consultations take place every six months so that you can monitor your health status. Thus, oral problems can be released early. In some cases, the patient is referred to a specialized professional, such as the endodontist. "Endodontics is the specialty within Dentistry that takes care of the heart of the tooth, which we call dental pulp", defines a professional. This part of the dental pulp is located from the dentin to the root of the teeth, being formed by nerves and blood vessels.
